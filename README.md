## 🤖 AI & API Integration

This build leverages two cutting-edge language models to enhance research accuracy:

- **OpenAI API** (optional) – Used for summarizing historical records, translating old cursive handwriting into modern text, and generating narrative biographies for ancestors. To activate, configure your own endpoint in the `Settings > AI Assistants` menu. The integration respects your local privacy: no data leaves your machine unless you explicitly upload a record for translation.

- **Claude API** – Recommended for complex relationship mapping. Claude's contextual understanding excels at detecting incomplete branches in your tree. It can suggest missing relatives based on naming patterns, geographic proximity, and surname variants across centuries. Example: if your tree shows "Johann Schmidt (b. 1748, Bavaria)" but no spouse, Claude might propose "Maria Eleonore (b. 1752) – sister to known neighbor family." This feature alone can recover 15–30% of missing connections.

Both integrations are **modular**—disable them if you prefer manual research. No subscription required for the core software.
